You've Been "Quished"!

Actually, you're safe—this was just a security awareness test. But what if this QR code had been placed by a hacker?

How to stay safe:
  • Inspect the source: Hackers often paste fake QR codes over legitimate ones (like on parking meters).
  • Check the URL: Your phone shows a preview before you click. Does it look official?
  • Never "Login" via QR: Avoid entering passwords on sites you reached via a random scan.
